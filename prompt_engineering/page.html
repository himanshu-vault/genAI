RiskIntelliScore™ is a cutting-edge predictive analytics tool designed for early risk detection in corporate client portfolios. It is engineered to identify financial distress, compliance violations, and operational anomalies, making it an essential tool for compliance teams, credit analysts, and enterprise risk officers. The product features a real-time scoring engine based on gradient-boosted decision trees, allowing for rapid and accurate risk assessment. It supports multi-source data ingestion, handling both structured and unstructured data, and integrates seamlessly with existing enterprise data pipelines and reporting dashboards. RiskIntelliScore™ is compatible with JSON and Parquet formats and can be deployed across major cloud platforms including Azure, GCP, and AWS. The product is ISO 27001 and SOC 2 Type II certified, ensuring robust compliance and audit capabilities, and supports GDPR and CCPA compliance.
